Mathly

I'm used to JavaScript, where you feed numbers to a math object.   In Ruby, math methods are native to numbers.

x = -4

x.abs  returns the absolute value of x.

rand  returns a random number less than one, and greater than or equal to zero.

rand(i)  returns a random number less than i, and greater than or equal to zero.

rand  can also be seeded. srand(i)  returns a predictable series of random numbers based on the value of i.
